What this site collects today

The booking inquiries on this preview may ask for name, email, appointment details, allergies, shoot context, and free-text notes—only what someone chooses to submit. Until you integrate a CRM, scheduler, email API, or analytics toolkit, submissions do not transmit to servers from this demo build.

Why we collect it

When wired for production, we use inquiries to reply with availability, consult questions, allergy screening, and NDAs aligned to brand work—not for unrelated resale.
