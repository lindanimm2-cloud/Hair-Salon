Privacy policy

Auburn Atelier (“we,” “us”) respects the information you share when you browse this site or request an appointment. This page explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

What we collect

Booking inquiries ask for your name, email, appointment details, allergies, and any notes you choose to include. We collect only what you submit—there is no background tracking beyond what is needed to serve the pages.

Why we collect it

Your details are used to reply with availability, prepare consult questions, and screen for allergies. They are never sold or used for unrelated marketing.

How it is shared

Information stays within the studio and the tools needed to run it—email, scheduling, and payment services. It is disclosed beyond that only when the law requires it.

Retention

Color formulas and consult notes are kept while you remain a client so results stay consistent between appointments. You can ask for them to be deleted at any time.

Security

Communication with the studio uses encrypted connections, inbox access is limited to the stylist, and photo releases are honored exactly as written—images are never published without your signed consent.

Your choices

Your personal information is handled in line with South Africa’s Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your records at any time through the booking contact.
  • Opt out of any newsletter or SMS updates the moment they stop being useful.
  • Decline photography entirely—your appointment is unaffected.

Children

Services are designed for adults. Appointments for minors require a parent or legal guardian to book and sign any releases.

Updates

Last updated July 2026. Material changes to this policy will be posted here with a new date.
